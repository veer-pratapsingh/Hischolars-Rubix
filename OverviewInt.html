Top ScholarshipsThe Inlaks Shivdasani Scholarships

The Inlaks Shivdasani Foundation Scholarships can be availed by meritorious Indian students

studying in universities in the UK, the US and in other European countries. The program provides

grants, awards, and scholarships to students doing full-time Masters, M.Phil or Doctoral degree

courses.

The foundation covers each recipient’s tuition fees as well as other expenses like living and travel

costs.

The scholarship amount is given just once and the Foundation does not allocate more than

USD 100,000.

This scholarship program is applicable for following given programs:
Inlaks Fine Arts Awards
Inlaks Fellowship for Indian Classical Music
Inlaks Theatre Awards
Eligibility:

The candidate must be an Indian citizen, and residing in India for at least six months at the

time of application.

No candidate should be over the age of 30 years
Hold a first-class undergraduate degree from a recognized university in India
Have fulfilled all the admission requirements of the chosen coursework

Also, the applicant must NOT hold a master’s degree from an international university. The

applicant must also not have already begun a postgraduate degree course abroad at the time

of application.
